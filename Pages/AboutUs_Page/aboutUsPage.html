About UniResidence

We know that choosing the right dorm can make all the difference in the world for college students. That's why we've created a community-driven platform that's designed to help students make informed decisions when it comes to selecting the perfect dorm.


UniResidence is your resource for real, honest dorm reviews from students just like you. We encourage you to share your experiences so that others can benefit from your insights. Join our community today and contribute to the conversation about college dorms.
